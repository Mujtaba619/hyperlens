Our Managed GRC & Risk services provide continuous oversight and intelligent automation, freeing you to focus on your core business objectives with confidence.

Comprehensive Support

Our End-to-End Managed GRC & Risk Services

Hyper Lens offers a complete suite of managed services designed to provide continuous, expert-led GRC and risk management. We don't just help you achieve certifications; we partner with you to sustain a robust and resilient compliance posture.

  • Continuous Compliance Monitoring: Real-time tracking of your adherence to various standards like FISMA, FedRAMP, HIPAA, SOC 2, PCI DSS, ISO 27001, CMMC, GDPR, and SOX.
  • Proactive Risk Identification & Mitigation: Utilizing AI and expert analysis to identify potential risks before they escalate, and implementing effective mitigation strategies.
  • Audit Readiness & Support: Ensuring you are always prepared for audits with organized documentation, mock audits, and expert guidance throughout the audit process.
  • Policy Lifecycle Management: Assisting in the development, implementation, communication, and ongoing review of your GRC policies.
  • Integrated GRC & MSP Synergy: Our GRC insights inform our Managed Security Services (MSSP) and IT operational support, ensuring that your systems not only perform optimally but also remain continuously compliant and secure. We don't just certify; we secure and sustain.
