Navigating the Evolving Regulatory Maze with Our Systems

Hyper Lens provides unmatched certification expertise across a wide range of demanding standards. Our AI-driven platform offers unified visibility, helping you achieve and maintain compliance efficiently and sure.

  • FISMA vs FedRAMP: FISMA sets federal IT security standards for all agencies; FedRAMP is specifically for cloud products and services used by federal agencies. We guide both paths clearly for you.
  • NIST vs ISO: NIST provides cybersecurity frameworks and guidelines, primarily for U.S. organizations and federal systems. ISO (e.g., ISO 27001) offers international standards for information security management systems (ISMS). We map controls and help integrate for broad or specific needs.
  • SOC 2 vs HIPAA: SOC 2 reports on controls at a service organization relevant to security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, or privacy. HIPAA specifically protects health information. We align security controls to satisfy both, if applicable, ensuring robust data safeguards.
  • ISO27001 vs SOC 2: ISO 27001 specifies an ISMS, a holistic approach to info security. SOC 2 is an attestation report on specific controls. We help build your ISMS for ISO 27001 and then leverage it for streamlined SOC 2 reporting, showcasing your strong posture to all key clients.
  • CMMC (All Levels): For Defense Industrial Base (DIB) contractors, CMMC ensures protection of CUI. We provide end-to-end guidance, from gap analysis to certification readiness for all required CMMC levels. This is vital for your full access.
  • PCI DSS: Essential for organizations handling cardholder data. Our methodology simplifies achieving and maintaining PCI DSS compliance, securing transactions and building trust with your clients now always.
  • GDPR & CCPA: Navigating complex data privacy regulations like GDPR (Europe) and CCPA (California) is crucial. We help implement necessary processes and controls to ensure compliance and protect user privacy fully.
  • SOX & GLBA: For financial integrity and corporate governance (SOX) or financial institution data protection (GLBA), we offer tailored solutions to meet these stringent regulatory demands of the total market.
  • ITAR & FIPS 140-2: We guide defense export controls (ITAR) and cryptographic module validation (FIPS 140-2), ensuring sensitive data and technologies are handled with utmost security and in full regulatory compliance too.
