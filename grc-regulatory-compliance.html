Framework Focus

Deep Dive into Key Regulatory Frameworks

Hyper Lens provides a comprehensive solution for achieving and maintaining FedRAMP authorization. Our platform automates control mapping, evidence collection, and continuous monitoring, integrating with cloud services like Azure to streamline the process. We guide you through System Security Plan (SSP) development, readiness assessments, and 3PAO audits.
For defense contractors, CMMC 2.0 is paramount. Hyper Lens offers tailored solutions for all CMMC levels. We assist with gap analysis, policy development, technical control implementation, and preparation for C3PAO assessments, ensuring your organization meets DoD requirements for protecting CUI.
Demonstrate your commitment to security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy with SOC 2. Hyper Lens helps you define scope, identify controls, gather evidence, and manage the audit process, leading to a successful attestation report.
For healthcare organizations and their business associates, protecting PHI is critical. Hyper Lens supports HIPAA and HITRUST compliance through robust security controls, risk assessments, policy management, and audit readiness, powered by our AI-driven GRC platform.
Hyper Lens assists organizations in adopting and implementing various NIST frameworks, including the Cybersecurity Framework (CSF), SP 800-53, and SP 800-171. We help you align your security practices with these globally recognized standards for enhanced cyber resilience.
