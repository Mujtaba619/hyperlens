Managing Third Party Risk

Your organization's security is only as strong as its weakest link.

In an interconnected world, third-party vendors, suppliers, and partners are integral to business operations. However, they also introduce significant risks. Hyper Lens provides a futuristic, AI-driven Third-Party Risk Management (TPRM) solution to help you identify, assess, mitigate, and monitor risks across your entire extended enterprise. Ensure your vendors meet your security and compliance standards (FedRAMP, CMMC, SOC 2, HIPAA) without overwhelming your resources.

Challenge: Limited Vendor Visibility Hyper Lens Solution: Continuous AI Monitoring
Challenge: Manual, Time-Consuming Assessments Hyper Lens Solution: Automated Questionnaires & AI Analysis
TPRM Solutions
Our Approach

The Hyper Lens AI-Powered TPRM Framework

  • 1. Intelligent Onboarding & Tiering

    Efficiently onboard vendors and use AI to classify them based on inherent risk, access to sensitive data, business impact, and compliance requirements.

  • 2. Automated Risk Assessment

    Deploy tailored questionnaires and leverage AI to analyze responses, external threat intelligence, and compliance attestations (e.g., SOC 2 reports).

  • 3. Continuous Monitoring & Alerts

    Our AI constantly monitors your third-party ecosystem for changes in risk posture, new vulnerabilities, or adverse events, providing real-time alerts.

Proactive Risk Mitigation

Hyper Lens's AI doesn't just identify current risks; it predicts potential future threats from your third parties. Stay ahead of supply chain attacks and vendor-related breaches.

Demonstrate robust due diligence and meet regulatory requirements (e.g., CMMC supply chain controls, HIPAA Business Associate Agreements) with comprehensive vendor assessments and audit trails. Our platform helps map vendor controls to your specific compliance needs, simplifying reporting.

  • Streamlined evidence gathering for audits.
  • Clear documentation of vendor risk management activities.
  • Support for various industry and government mandates.
