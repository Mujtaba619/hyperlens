Master Your Cloud Security with Hyper Lens CSPM

As organizations increasingly migrate to the cloud (Azure, AWS, GCP, hybrid), maintaining a robust security posture becomes paramount. Hyper Lens offers advanced Cloud Security Posture Management (CSPM) solutions, powered by AI, to provide continuous visibility, identify misconfigurations, assess risks, and automate compliance across your multi-cloud environments. Ensure your cloud infrastructure is secure, compliant, and resilient against evolving threats.

Multi-Cloud Support Azure, AWS, GCP & More
Continuous Monitoring Real-Time Threat Detection
Compliance Automation FedRAMP, SOC 2, HIPAA
Core Capabilities

What Our AI-Driven CSPM Delivers

Comprehensive Visibility

Gain a unified view of all cloud assets, configurations, and security policies across your entire multi-cloud estate. Integrates with Azure Advisor and other native cloud tools.

Misconfiguration Detection

AI-powered scans continuously identify security misconfigurations, deviations from best practices, and policy violations that could expose your cloud resources.

Automated Compliance

Map cloud controls to regulatory frameworks like FedRAMP, CMMC, SOC 2, HIPAA, and ISO 27001. Automate evidence gathering and reporting for audits.

Your Advantages

Benefits of Choosing Hyper Lens for Cloud Security

Proactively identify and remediate vulnerabilities and misconfigurations that malicious actors could exploit. Our AI provides early warnings of potential attack vectors, enhancing your cloud's defensibility.
