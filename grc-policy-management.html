Transform Your Policy Lifecycle with Hyper Lens

From Creation to Attestation: A Seamless, AI-Driven Approach

Effective policy management is the cornerstone of a strong GRC program. Hyper Lens provides a centralized, AI-powered platform to streamline the entire policy lifecycle. Say goodbye to scattered documents, manual tracking, and compliance gaps. Our solution ensures your policies are current, communicated, understood, and consistently enforced across your organization.

Challenge: Disorganized policy versions and lack of central control.

Solution: Unified repository with version control and audit trails.

Challenge: Ensuring employee awareness and acknowledgment.

Solution: Automated distribution, training, and attestation workflows.

Our Advantage

Key Benefits of Hyper Lens Policy Management

Centralized Repository

Store all policies and procedures in a single, secure, and easily accessible location. Ensure everyone is working with the most current versions.

Automated Workflows

Streamline policy creation, review, approval, distribution, and attestation processes with intelligent automation. Reduce manual tasks and improve efficiency.

Version Control & Audit Trails

Maintain a complete history of policy changes, reviews, and approvals. Easily track versions and demonstrate compliance with detailed audit logs for any regulation (FedRAMP, SOC 2 etc.).

Our Process

The Hyper Lens Policy Lifecycle Management

Step 1

Create

Develop new policies or import existing ones using intuitive templates and AI-assisted drafting. Collaborate with stakeholders seamlessly.

Step 2

Approve

Configure custom approval workflows. Route policies for review and sign-off by designated individuals or groups, with clear tracking.

Step 3

Distribute

Automate policy dissemination to relevant employees, departments, or groups. Ensure targeted communication for maximum impact.

Step 4

Attest

Track employee acknowledgments and attestations. Generate reports to demonstrate policy awareness and acceptance across the organization.

Step 5

Review & Update

Set automated review cycles. Our AI helps identify outdated policies or those needing updates based on regulatory changes or internal incidents.

Platform Capabilities

Advanced Features for Robust Policy Management

Hyper Lens goes beyond basic policy storage. Our platform is packed with features designed to make your policy management efficient, effective, and audit-ready.

  • AI-Powered Policy Suggestions: Get recommendations for policy updates based on new regulations or identified risks.
  • Cross-Referencing: Easily link policies to relevant regulations (FedRAMP, SOC 2, HIPAA controls) and internal controls.
  • Targeted Distribution Lists: Ensure policies reach the right audience with dynamic group management.
  • Customizable Attestation Campaigns: Tailor acknowledgment workflows to specific policies or employee groups.
  • Comprehensive Reporting: Generate reports on policy status, attestation rates, review cycles, and audit trails.
  • Integration with GRC Modules: Seamlessly connect policy management with risk assessment and incident response.
