Go Beyond Reporting: Unlock Predictive & Prescriptive Insights

Hyper Lens Analytics turns data into your most valuable strategic weapon.

Standard reporting shows you what happened. Hyper Lens Advanced Analytics, fueled by AI and machine learning, tells you why it happened, what will likely happen next, and what you should do about it. We process vast amounts of aggregated GRC, security (including Azure Advisor data), and MSP operational data to uncover hidden patterns, predict future risks and opportunities, and prescribe optimal actions.

This capability is crucial for maintaining compliance with dynamic standards like FedRAMP and CMMC, proactively managing cybersecurity threats, and optimizing IT service delivery for peak performance and cost-efficiency.
