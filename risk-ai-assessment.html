Our Methodology

The Hyper Lens AI Risk Assessment Lifecycle

1. Intelligent Data Fusion

Our AI platform ingests data from cloud (Azure, AWS, GCP), security tools, GRC systems, and logs, then intelligently contextualizes it for business processes, risk management, and compliance (FedRAMP, CMMC, SOC 2).

  • Automated connectors for seamless data flow.
  • Normalization of diverse data formats for unified analysis.
  • Mapping data to assets, controls, and regulatory requirements.

2. AI Analysis & Prediction

Hyper Lens's proprietary AI algorithms analyze the aggregated data to identify vulnerabilities, model potential threats, and predict the likelihood and impact of adverse events. We go beyond known signatures to uncover emerging risks.

  • Machine learning for anomaly detection and pattern recognition.
  • Predictive scoring of risks based on likelihood and impact.
  • Simulation of attack paths and potential business disruption.

3. Actionable Risk Insights

Our platform translates complex AI findings into clear, actionable insights. Risks are prioritized based on their potential business impact and alignment with your risk appetite, enabling you to focus resources effectively.

  • Clear dashboards visualizing risk posture and trends.
  • Prioritized remediation recommendations.
  • Integration with ticketing and GRC workflow tools for tracking.
