Hyper Lens Privacy Policy Use

Our Commitment to Your Data Privacy and Security Work.

1. Introduction: Our Privacy Pledge Now

Hyper Lens ("Hyper Lens," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website hyperlens.net (the "Site"), and use our futuristic Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) solutions, AI-powered Managed Services Provider (MSP) offerings, and the Hyper Lens platform (collectively, the "Services"). This policy covers data from Azure Advisor, cloud systems, and our interactions to ensure FedRAMP, CMMC, SOC 2, HIPAA, NIST, and ISO compliance support for our clients today.

2. Information We Collect From Users Here

We may collect personal identification information (e.g., name, email, phone), company information, GRC and MSP operational data you provide, usage data from our Site and Services (via cookies or analytics), and technical data related to your IT infrastructure when you use our MSP services. This includes data relevant to achieving certifications like FedRAMP, CMMC, SOC 2. We gather data from cloud systems like Azure Advisor, AWS, and GCP for our unified dashboard and AI insights when authorized by all clients. Our systems are safe for all that is true.

3. How We Use Your Information Today Good

Your information is used to provide and improve our Services, including delivering AI-driven GRC insights, managing your IT infrastructure (MSP), facilitating certifications (FedRAMP, CMMC), personalizing your experience, communicating with you, processing transactions, and for internal analytics to enhance our Hyper Lens platform and futuristic offerings. We analyze data from Azure Advisor and other cloud systems for GRC and operational intelligence. This ensures the best work from the team always for you.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure For Teams

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our Site and Services (e.g., cloud hosting like Azure, payment processors), or to comply with legal obligations (e.g., for FedRAMP or CMMC requirements if applicable), or with your explicit consent. All third parties are contractually obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your data. This is our promise and plan for GRC clients.

5. Data Security Measures In Place For Use

We implement a variety of robust security measures, including encryption, access controls, and regular security assessments, to maintain the safety of your personal information and sensitive GRC/MSP data, including information processed from Azure Advisor. Our commitment to security is paramount in our futuristic GRC and MSP delivery and for all of the users of the system. We aim to be the best in this key work.

6. Data Retention and Your Rights As Users

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law (e.g., for GRC audit trails for FedRAMP). You have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. Residents of certain jurisdictions (e.g., GDPR, CCPA) have additional rights. These are key to our plan for all.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies Made

Our Site may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and to collect information about how you use our Site and Services. This helps us improve our futuristic GRC and MSP platform offerings and tailor content. You can typically set your browser to refuse cookies, but some parts of the Site may not function properly. We use this for better client support as well for the main work.

8. Third-Party Website Links Provider Work

Our Site or Services may contain links to other websites not operated or controlled by Hyper Lens. This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party GRC or IT sites you visit. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these external websites. This is for your own good and safety while working.

9. Children's Online Privacy Protection OK

Our Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information from our GRC and MSP systems. This is our pledge to all users of the system we use for your good use.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy Is Okay

Hyper Lens reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will post any changes on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting the GRC and MSP information we collect from users. Your continued use of our Site or Services constitutes your agreement to this Privacy Policy and any future updates for work.

11. Contact Us About Your Data Privacy Now

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at: Hyper Lens, Global HQ Future Park, Innovation City, USA, Email: privacy@hyperlens.net, Phone: (1) 650-434-4829.
